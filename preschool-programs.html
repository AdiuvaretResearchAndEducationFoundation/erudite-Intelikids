Preschool Programs

Comprehensive Preschool Programs to Nurture Curiosity, Confidence, and Lifelong Learning.

Playgroup

Exploring the World Through Play

Our Playgroup program is designed for children aged 1.5 to 2.5 years. It focuses on helping them explore their surroundings, develop sensory and motor skills, and begin their journey of social interaction in a safe, fun, and nurturing environment. Activities include music, movement, and imaginative play to foster curiosity and joy in learning.

  • Exploration Driven by Sensory Experiences
  • Short Attention Span of 5-7 Minutes
  • Early Memory Formation and Milestones
  • Play-Based Learning for Development

Nursery

Building Foundations for Lifelong Learning

Our Nursery program caters to children aged 2.5 to 3.5 years. This stage emphasizes early literacy, numeracy, and creative thinking through structured play and interactive lessons. Activities focus on storytelling, art, and hands-on exploration, ensuring every child feels supported while building confidence and independence.

  • Increased Attention Span (10-12 Minutes)
  • Interactive Learning Activities
  • Focus on Cognitive and Language Skills
  • Development of Social-Emotional Intelligence

LKG

Preparing for a Bright Academic Journey

Our Lower Kindergarten (LKG) program is tailored for children aged 3.5 to 4.5 years. This phase introduces structured academics with a focus on reading, writing, numeracy, and communication skills. Children are encouraged to think critically, ask questions, and work collaboratively through engaging activities like group discussions, puzzles, and projects.

  • Introduction to Literacy and Numeracy Skills
  • Increased Focus Time for Learning
  • Strengthened Memory and Recall
  • Encouraging Creativity and Self-Expression

UKG

Building Confidence for Primary School

Our Upper Kindergarten (UKG) program is designed for children aged 4.5 to 5.5 years. This stage focuses on preparing children for primary school by enhancing problem-solving skills, fostering creativity, and encouraging teamwork. With a balanced mix of academics and co-curricular activities, children are equipped with a strong foundation for success in their future education.

  • Foundation for Primary School Transition
  • Introduction to Phonics and Early Reading
  • Advanced Math Concepts and Learning
  • Science Exploration for Academic Growth
